About us

BAE fm is an outdoor vibrant virtual online radio that was established from a township called Seshego in Polokwane, Limpopo Province. This is a Platform that grant an opportunity to the community to voice-out its challenges and achievements, being it in the Art and Entertainment industry, or any issues that affect the community on a daily basis. BAE fm empower, develop and foster skilled and unskilled young creatives who have interest in the Media industry and passionate journalists at a ground-level phase voluntary. Marketing and promoting small businesses & creative practitioner is one of its objectives that it values and embraces.

Broadcasts

Our unique outdoor broadcasting methodology create a vibrant and exciting environment in the society. This mobile broadcasting strategy allows us to be able reach any society in the country in a convenient way and reassuring that every citizen in South Africa get to share his/her views. BAE fm contain very energetic and vibrant presenters who broadcast utilizing all various official South African languages. Its plays various music genres locally & internationally that are massively enjoyed by our listeners and viewers – Afro-pop, Afro-jazz, R&B, Gospel, Reggae, Kwaito, Hip Hop, Pop from the 80’s,90’s and the latest. Its programme gun to educate, aspire, inspire and entertain.
